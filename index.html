How it works

One screen to watch. One page to play. No install required.

  1. 1 Show up for the stream

    Watch the run on Twitch, YouTube, or wherever the broadcast goes live.

  2. 2 Open dungeoncrawlers.tv

    When a decision window opens, the site lights up with buttons, votes, and limited-time actions.

  3. 3 Change what happens next

    The crowd's choices feed back into the game. The dungeon reacts on stream.
